Per Diem Online Schedule is an app that helps you craft a satisfying schedule.
A complete, thought out schedule is an artificially imposed constraint meant to help you focus on a set of goals that you would actually spend time trying to achieve.

Sheets

  • Schedule: This is where you log all the activities you did in a particular day. Along with goals for that day and notes.
  • Settings: Disable or create new activities, change activity colors, change graphs settings.
  • Data: Non interactive. This sheet contains Daily and Weekly averages for every enabled activity regardless if you want to graph it or not. You can make your own custom graphs with this data.
  • Graphs: All activities set to show on graph Will appear on the graphs. One will have the weekly averages doing a particular activity. The second one is an area graph of productive time vs. wasted time.

How to Use Satisfy Online Schedule

  • The schedule consists of an expandable schedule with colored cells that indicate a particular activity. Make the schedule bigger by pressing Expand Schedule
  • You can add to the list of recognized activities in the Settings sheet. Type a name, choose a color, and check the Enabled checkbox
  • Click on Update in the Satisfy AddOn menu to update all settings in the Settingssheet and update the data on the Data sheet.
  • You can start logging what you did today!
